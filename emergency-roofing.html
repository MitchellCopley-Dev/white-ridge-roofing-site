What to do if your roof is leaking right now?

  1. Don't Panic: Clear the area under the leak to protect your belongings.
  2. Contain the Water: Place buckets under the drip. If the ceiling is bulging, poke a small hole to drain the water (this prevents the ceiling from collapsing).
  3. Call White Ridge: Our emergency crew will arrive to secure the exterior and stop the source.
